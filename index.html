About Us

Nestled in the heart of Sacramento, Mystic Mediterranean Cuisine is a culinary haven where tradition meets innovation. From savory kebabs to flavorful falafels, each dish reflects our commitment to quality ingredients and time-honored techniques.

With warm hospitality and a cozy ambiance, we welcome you to experience the rich tapestry of Mediterranean flavors that have been cherished in our family for years. Join us at Mystic Mediterranean Cuisine, where every meal is a journey to the shores of the Mediterranean.

Dinner

Appetizers

  • Mystic Mezze Platter: A tempting array of hummus, baba ganoush, stuffed grape leaves, and warm pita bread.
  • Mediterranean Stuffed Mushrooms: Mushrooms stuffed with a savory blend of spinach, feta cheese, and herbs, baked to perfection.
  • Crispy Calamari: Lightly battered calamari rings served with tzatziki sauce and lemon wedges.
  • Grilled Octopus: Tender octopus marinated in olive oil and herbs, grilled to a smoky perfection, served with a citrus-infused dressing.

Salads

  • Greek Salad: Fresh lettuce, tomatoes, cucumbers, red onions, Kalamata olives, and feta cheese tossed in a lemon-herb vinaigrette.
  • Fattoush Salad: A refreshing mix of crispy pita chips, tomatoes, cucumbers, radishes, and fresh herbs, dressed in a tangy sumac vinaigrette.

Main Course

  • Lamb Souvlaki Platter: Juicy lamb skewers served with grilled vegetables, rice pilaf, and tzatziki sauce.
  • Seafood Paella: A classic Spanish dish with a Mediterranean twist, featuring shrimp, mussels, calamari, and chorizo simmered with saffron-infused rice.
  • Vegetarian Moussaka: Layers of eggplant, potatoes, and zucchini baked with a rich tomato sauce and topped with creamy béchamel sauce.
  • Grilled Sea Bass: Fresh sea bass fillet marinated in lemon and herbs, grilled to perfection, served with roasted potatoes and seasonal vegetables.

Dessert

  • Baklava: Layers of flaky pastry filled with nuts and sweetened with honey syrup.
